ABOUT ASM AUTO REPAIRS

Your Local Mobile Mechanic

ASM Auto Repairs is a mobile mechanic service based in Braybrook, Melbourne, servicing vehicles at your home, workplace, or roadside. Operated by Andy, ASM Auto Repairs brings professional-grade automotive repairs and servicing directly to you — saving you the hassle of dropping your car off at a workshop.

With a focus on honesty, reliability, and quality workmanship, ASM Auto Repairs offers a wide range of mechanical services for most makes and models across Melbourne's western suburbs.

WHAT WE DO

Our Services

Professional mobile mechanical services delivered to your home, workplace or roadside across Melbourne's western suburbs.

Logbook Servicing

Manufacturer-scheduled logbook services to keep your warranty valid and your car running at its best.

General Mechanical Repairs

From minor fixes to full mechanical overhauls — we handle all general repairs at your location.

Brake Repairs & Replacement

Brake pads, rotors, calipers and brake fluid — safety-critical work done on-site with quality parts.

Roadworthy Certificates (RWC)

Licensed roadworthy inspections to certify your vehicle meets Victorian road safety standards.

Vehicle Diagnostics & Fault Scanning

Electronic fault scanning and diagnostics to accurately identify warning lights and hidden issues.

Tyre Replacement & Rotation

New tyre fitting and rotation for even tread wear, improved handling, and longer tyre life.

Battery Replacement

Battery testing and replacement at your location — no need to worry about a car that won't start.

Clutch Repairs

Clutch plate, pressure plate and flywheel repairs for manual transmissions — done properly, first time.

Suspension & Steering

Shock absorbers, struts, tie rods and steering components checked and replaced for safe, smooth driving.

Engine Repairs

Diagnostics and hands-on engine repairs covering a wide range of makes, models, and fault types.

Pre-Purchase Vehicle Inspections

Buying a used car? We inspect the vehicle thoroughly before you commit so you buy with confidence.

Roadside Assistance

Stuck on the road? Andy comes to you for emergency roadside repairs and breakdown assistance.
